Solo Mining Odds

What are the odds of finding a block entirely on your own? Think of it as a lottery ticket.

Pool mining gives you steady, small payouts proportional to your hashrate. You share block rewards with the pool.

Solo mining: you keep the full block reward (3.125 BTC = 312,500,000 sats) if you find a block. The odds are slim, but real.
